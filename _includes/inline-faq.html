• What types of policies does the Ounce of Prevention promote?
  • We promote policies that: expand access to high-quality early childhood education programs, build sustainable early childhood systems supported by private-public partnerships, emphasize parents as children's first teachers, ensure programs are staffed by a well-trained workforce, and tend to the physical, cognitive and social-emotional needs of young children.
  • Does the Ounce of Prevention host events?
  • The Ounce of Prevention Fund hosts several events throughout the year for civic leaders, advocates, early childhood professionals, and supporters.
